人生发财真的靠康波吗？

Does Wealth Really Follow the Kondratieff Cycle?

"周期天王"周金涛 2016 年的演讲让"人生发财靠康波"成为中文金融圈的流行语。本文用 1995–2024 年的资产数据检验他指认的三个"机会窗口"——以及康波理论本身。

A 2016 speech by Chinese economist Zhou Jintao made "wealth comes from the Kondratieff wave" a popular slogan in Chinese finance. We test his three claimed "wealth windows" — and the underlying theory itself — against asset data from 1995 to 2024.

结论先行 Bottom Line

周金涛指认的三个"康波机会窗口"（1999、2008、2019）在四类主要资产中没有一个是真正的最优买入时点。A 股 2008 年初买入持有 10 年年化为 −4.5%（亏损）；美股 1999 年初买入持有 10 年年化 −3.0%（亏损）。本文做了五个独立的量化检验：CAGR 排名、t / Mann-Whitney / Bootstrap p 值（全部 > 0.05）、Sharpe 比率、最大回撤、以及 155 年美国 GDP 频谱分析（60 年频率 p = 0.69）。没有一项支持康波"机会窗口"作为可执行投资信号。

Zhou Jintao's three claimed "Kondratieff wealth windows" (1999, 2008, 2019) are not the actual optimal buy points for any of the four major asset classes tested. Buying the Shanghai Composite at the start of 2008 yielded −4.5% CAGR over 10 years; the S&P 500 at the start of 1999 yielded −3.0%. We run five independent quantitative tests: CAGR rank, t / Mann-Whitney / Bootstrap p-values (all > 0.05), Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, and 155-year US GDP spectral analysis (p = 0.69 at the 60-year frequency). None supports treating Kondratieff "windows" as actionable investment signals.

This is good — it gives us concrete years to test. The question becomes: if you bought at the start of these "windows" and held for 10 years, did you outperform the average buy year across major asset classes?

方法 Method

检验对象：1999、2008、2019 三个"康波机会窗口"年初买入。对照组：1995–2014 年间所有 20 个候选起点（保证 10 年回报可计算到 2024 年；北京房因数据自 1999 年起仅 16 个起点）。

Treated: buying at the start of 1999, 2008, and 2019 (the three claimed windows). Control: all 20 candidate start years from 1995 to 2014 (10-year returns computable through 2024; Beijing housing has only 16 start years, since data begins in 1999).

资产：四类周金涛理论中应受康波驱动的核心资产——A 股（上证综指[3]）、美股（S&P 500[4]）、北京住宅均价（统计年鉴[5]）、黄金（LBMA 美元金价[6]）。

Assets: four asset classes Zhou's framework explicitly identifies as Kondratieff-driven — Chinese A-shares (Shanghai Composite[3]), US equities (S&P 500[4]), Beijing housing[5], and gold[6].

结果变量：年初买入并持有 10 年的复合年化收益率（CAGR）。仅统计价格回报，不含股息与房租收入——这对所有起点同等处理，不影响相对比较。

Outcome: 10-year compound annual growth rate (CAGR) from January of the start year. Price returns only, excluding dividends and rental income — applied uniformly to all start years, so relative comparisons hold.

频谱分析：60 年周期真的存在吗？ Spectral Analysis: Does the 60-Year Cycle Even Exist?

前面五节都是给定康波理论存在的前提下，检验它的具体预测能不能站得住。但更根本的问题是：所谓"60 年周期"在数据中真的存在吗？

The previous five sections tested specific predictions assuming the Kondratieff cycle exists. The more fundamental question is: does a "60-year cycle" actually exist in the data at all?

回答这个问题的标准量化金融工具是频谱分析（spectral analysis）——把时间序列分解成不同频率的正弦波叠加，看哪些频率的能量（功率）最大。如果康波理论成立，60 年这个频率上应该有显著的功率峰值。

The standard quantitative finance tool for this is spectral analysis — decomposing a time series into sinusoidal components at different frequencies and measuring the power (energy) at each. If Kondratieff theory holds, there should be a significant power peak at the 60-year frequency.

我们对美国 1870–2024 年实际 GDP 增长率序列（共 155 年，足够容纳 2.5 个完整康波）做线性去趋势化后，应用 Welch 功率谱估计[15]。结果如下：

学界证据并非一边倒——这个结果如何与现有文献协调

The Literature Is Not One-Sided — How This Result Fits Within It

必须诚实承认：康波频谱检验在学界并非一致结论。两派研究都存在：

In fairness: spectral tests of Kondratieff cycles are not unanimous in the literature. Two camps exist:

支持康波存在的研究： Bieshaar & Kleinknecht (1984) 在 Konjunkturpolitik 上对 1890 年以来六组长波时间表做了 econometric test，结论是 "统计证据 quite strong and robust"[20]。Korotayev & Tsirel (2010) 用 1870–2007 全球 GDP 数据做频谱分析，发现 ≈52 年频率峰值[21]。

Studies supporting K-waves: Bieshaar & Kleinknecht (1984) tested six long-wave chronologies on multiple time series since 1890 and concluded that "statistical evidence is quite strong and robust"[20]. Korotayev & Tsirel (2010) ran spectral analysis on 1870–2007 global GDP and reported a ≈52-year peak[21].

否定康波存在的研究： Solomou (1989, Cambridge UP) 系统检验 1850–1973 年主要资本主义经济体增长数据，"长波难以从数据中识别"[8]。Diebolt & Doliger (2006, 2008) 用频谱分析 OECD 多国 GDP 数据，未找到稳健的 50–60 年周期，但确认了 Kuznets 周期 (≈15–20 年)[22]。Metz (2011, Cliometrica) 综述了所有时序方法的争议，结论是这一问题对方法选择极为敏感[23]。

Studies rejecting K-waves: Solomou (1989, Cambridge UP) systematically tested 1850–1973 growth data across major capitalist economies and concluded that "long waves are not identifiable in the data"[8]. Diebolt & Doliger (2006, 2008) ran spectral analysis on OECD GDP data and found no robust 50–60 year cycle while confirming Kuznets swings (≈15–20 years)[22]. Metz (2011, Cliometrica) reviewed the methodological debate and concluded the result is highly sensitive to the choice of method[23].

为什么各研究结论不同？ 关键差异在于三个方法学选择：(1) 用 GDP 水平还是 GDP 增长率；(2) 去趋势用线性还是 HP filter / 多项式；(3) 是否做随机化检验。我们用的方法（增长率 + 线性去趋势 + Bootstrap）是偏保守的——它对真实的低频信号也较难识别。即便如此，主流经济学接受的 Juglar 和 Kuznets 周期都被识别出来；而康波频率没有。换言之：用同一个保守方法对同一个序列检验，能识别出已知的中频周期，却找不到康波——这本身就是有意义的对照。

Why do studies disagree? The key differences are three methodological choices: (1) GDP levels vs. growth rates; (2) linear vs. HP-filter vs. polynomial detrending; (3) whether randomization tests are performed. Our approach (growth rates + linear detrending + bootstrap) is conservative — it is less likely to detect even genuine low-frequency signals. Yet the same conservative method, applied to the same series, recovers the well-accepted Juglar and Kuznets cycles, but not Kondratieff. Identifying known cycles while failing to identify Kondratieff using the same conservative pipeline is itself the informative contrast.

更关键的是：即使你接受康波存在（采纳 Bieshaar-Kleinknecht 阵营），它能解释的是宏观长波——比如美国 GDP 在 1890–2024 间的低频起伏。它不能用来推论"个人在 1999、2008、2019 年买入特定资产能成为中产"这种具体可执行命题。宏观长波存在 ≠ 60 年里恰好有 3 个可识别的"机会窗口" ≠ 这些窗口能被事先准确定位。

More crucially: even if you accept K-waves exist (the Bieshaar-Kleinknecht camp), they describe macro long waves — low-frequency oscillations in something like US GDP from 1890 to 2024. They cannot license the much more specific claim that "buying particular assets in 1999, 2008, or 2019 will let you join the middle class." The existence of macro long waves does not entail that there are exactly 3 identifiable "opportunity windows" per 60 years, much less that they can be located in advance.

方向值得注意：全部 4 个差异都是负值——意味着按周金涛指认的"机会窗口"买入，统计上略差于随机选一年。这个差距虽然不显著（样本太小），但方向上完全不支持流言。

The direction is striking: all four differences are negative — buying at Zhou's "windows" was statistically slightly worse than buying in a random year. The gap isn't significant (samples too small), but the direction directly contradicts the myth.

关于n = 2 的样本问题：周金涛只指认了 1999 和 2008 两个有完整 10 年回报的窗口。因此一个公平的批评是：3 个观测下任何方法都难以达到统计显著。我们承认这点，但反向论证同样成立——如果康波理论是真的，它应该至少在方向上把这 2 个观测推向正向差异，而不是负向。

On the sample size: Zhou identified only two windows (1999 and 2008) with complete 10-year horizons. A fair critique is that no method can reach significance with n = 2. We accept that — but the reverse logic also holds: if the theory were real, it should at least push these two observations in the positive direction, not the negative.

风险调整收益与最大回撤 Risk-Adjusted Returns & Max Drawdown

单看 CAGR 没有充分体现"机会窗口"承诺的内涵——它真正承诺的是低风险、高回报的入场点。我们用两个标准量化指标检验这点。

CAGR alone doesn't capture what the "opportunity window" really promises — that is, a low-risk, high-reward entry point. Two standard quantitative metrics test this directly.

夏普比率（Sharpe Ratio）

Sharpe Ratio

Maximum Drawdown

康波理论说"机会窗口"是低点入场。如果真的是低点，那么之后的最大回撤应该小于基准均值。

频谱分析否定了"60 年周期"的存在性 (p = 0.69)；事件研究的 12 个 p 值全部 > 0.05；最大回撤检验显示窗口入场反而风险更高。三个独立的量化金融工具，三个不一致的方向都指向同一结论。

Spectral analysis rejects the existence of a 60-year cycle (p = 0.69); 12 p-values from event-study tests all exceed 0.05; max-drawdown analysis shows window entries carry higher downside risk. Three independent quantitative finance tools, all pointing the same direction.

康波理论本身：学界争议 The Theory Itself: A Divided Literature

即使把"周金涛预测准不准"放一边，"康波周期"作为科学命题本身也面临严重的实证问题。但需要诚实承认：学界对康波是否存在并非一边倒——既有支持的实证研究（§ 06 已介绍），也有反对的。我们这里聚焦反对方的核心论点。

Even setting aside Zhou's specific predictions, the Kondratieff wave as a scientific claim faces serious empirical problems. But to be honest: the literature is not one-sided — there are empirical studies on both sides (some discussed in § 06). Here we focus on the critical case.

三条具体的实证批评

Three specific empirical critiques

1. 时序方法问题。剑桥大学经济学家 Solomos Solomou 在 1989 年的《1850–1973 年经济增长阶段》[8] 中系统检验了主要资本主义经济体的长期增长数据，结论是康波"难以从数据中识别出"——他特别指出 Kondratieff 原始论文中"对数据拟合一条确定性时间趋势线"的方法假设了趋势平稳过程的存在，而真实经济时序"更可能是随机过程"[9]。换句话说：他看到的"周期"很可能只是把单调趋势剔除后剩余噪声里的几次随机起伏。

1. Time-series methodology. Cambridge economist Solomos Solomou's 1989 monograph Phases of Economic Growth, 1850–1973[8] systematically tested major capitalist economies' long-run growth data and concluded that long waves are "not identifiable" in the data. He specifically noted that Kondratieff's original method of fitting a deterministic time trend assumed a trend-stationary growth path that "is unlikely to hold over long periods" — real economic series "are best depicted as stochastic rather than deterministic processes"[9]. Translation: the "cycles" he saw were likely just a few random fluctuations in residuals left over after removing a single monotonic trend.

2. Kuczynski 自己的检验。1978 年 Kuczynski 用频谱分析检验全球工业产值、专利数等多个序列，他自己的结论是"我们无法排除这个 60 年周期是一个随机周期的可能"[10]。注意这是支持长波理论的研究者自己写的——他从未对统计显著性做正式检验。

2. Kuczynski's own caveat. In 1978, Kuczynski applied spectral analysis to global industrial output, patents, and other series — he himself wrote that "we cannot exclude the possibility that the 60-year cycle … is a random cycle"[10]. Notably, this is from a researcher sympathetic to long-wave theory; he never performed a formal test of statistical significance.

3. 样本量根本不够。从 18 世纪工业革命开始算到现在约 250 年，最多只能容纳 4–5 个完整康波。要在统计学上拒绝"这是噪声"的零假设，需要至少 30+ 个独立周期才能达到 p < 0.05。我们离这个样本量还差一个数量级——几代人的时间内都不可能补足。Rothbard 直接称康波为"最虚的'周期'"，并指出它在最多三个半周期里看起来才像周期[11]。

3. The sample size is fundamentally inadequate. From the start of the Industrial Revolution to now is about 250 years, fitting at most 4–5 complete K-waves. Rejecting the null of "this is noise" at p < 0.05 typically requires 30+ independent cycles. We are an order of magnitude short — and won't reach sufficient sample for several human generations. Rothbard called the Kondratieff cycle "the flimsiest 'cycle' of them all," noting it appears regular only across at most three-and-a-half periods[11].

为什么这个流言这么有传播力 Why the Myth Spreads So Well

数据已经否定了具体预测、学界已经否定了一般理论，但这个说法还在 2024–2026 年的中文社交媒体上反复流传。这背后有四个机制——比上一期"巴菲特现金堆"流言多一个，也更值得深思。

The data refute the specific predictions, the academic literature refutes the general theory — yet the claim continues circulating on Chinese-language social media in 2024–2026. Four mechanisms are at work — one more than the Buffett myth, and worth dwelling on.

  1. 幸存者偏差与事后诸葛亮

    Survivorship bias & hindsight reasoning

    人们记住的是周金涛 2007 年"预测了次贷危机"的那次出名——并以此为基础接受他后续所有判断。但任何长期发表数百份研报的策略分析师都会有"命中"的时刻。同时段做出相似预测的还有 Roubini、Schiff 等，但他们后续的判断很多也没应验。中文金融圈选择性地记住周的成功（2007）而忽略他实际上失败的具体预测（2008 中国"机会"、2019 "85 后机会"等）。

    People remember Zhou's 2007 "prediction of the subprime crisis" and accept his subsequent claims on that basis. But any strategist publishing hundreds of reports over years will have hits. Roubini, Schiff, and others made similar 2007 predictions; many of their later calls didn't pan out either. Chinese-language finance media selectively remember Zhou's hit (2007) and ignore his actual misses (2008 China "opportunity," 2019 "post-1985 first window," etc.).

  2. 宿命论的心理舒适

    The comfort of fatalism

    "人生发财靠康波"在表面上是一个金融理论，但在心理层面它提供了一种解脱——如果财富积累不靠个人努力而靠时代周期，那么没致富的人就不必为自己负责，致富的人也不必感激。这种宿命论框架在中国房价高企、青年职业焦虑剧增的 2017 年之后特别有市场。它不是流言广传的副产品，而是流言广传的核心驱动力。Rothbard 称康波理论"带有邪教气质"[11]，原因之一就是它满足了对"宏大解释框架"的心理需求。

    "Life's wealth comes from the wave" is nominally a financial theory, but psychologically it offers relief — if wealth flows from cyclical fate rather than personal effort, those who didn't get rich aren't accountable, and those who did don't owe gratitude. This fatalist frame found especially fertile ground in China after 2017, amid soaring housing prices and intensifying youth career anxiety. It is not a side effect of the myth's popularity but the engine of it. Rothbard described Kondratieff theory as having a "cult-like" quality[11] — partly because it satisfies the demand for grand explanatory frames.

  3. 过度灵活的理论

    An over-flexible theory

    康波理论的"周期长度 50–60 年"留有 ±10 年弹性，"机会窗口"的判定也没有严格量化标准。任何资产价格行为事后都可以被归入"回升""繁荣""衰退""萧条"四个阶段中的某一个——这是 Karl Popper 所批评的不可证伪理论的典型特征[12]。Rothbard 也指出："不同周期理论家对长波年份给出不同纪事（chronologies），缺乏共识"[13]。一个允许多种相互冲突的"正确答案"的理论，本质上不可能被错。

    Kondratieff theory allows ±10 years of flexibility around its "50–60 year" period, and there is no quantitative criterion for what counts as an "opportunity window." Any asset price behavior can be retroactively assigned to one of the four phases — recovery, prosperity, recession, depression. This is the classic feature Karl Popper called unfalsifiability[12]. Different long-wave theorists give "different chronologies" with no consensus[13]. A theory that admits multiple mutually inconsistent "right answers" cannot, in principle, be wrong.

  4. 遗作崇拜与早逝光环

    Posthumous halo

    周金涛 2016 年 12 月去世，享年 44 岁。他的演讲在他去世后一年才整理成书《涛动周期论》并大规模流传。早逝、英才、"一句名言"——这些元素让对其理论的批评在情感上变得困难。但对一个理论的同情和对一个人的同情是两件事。理论必须经得起数据检验，无论它的提出者是谁。

    Zhou died in December 2016 at age 44. His speeches were compiled posthumously into a book a year later, and only then went viral. The combination — young, brilliant, gone too soon, "memorable phrase" — makes critique of his theory emotionally difficult. But sympathy for the person is not the same as sympathy for the theory. A theory must withstand data tests regardless of who proposed it.

什么是诚实的版本 A More Honest Version

"宏观环境会显著影响个人财富积累"——这个命题是对的。1980–2010 年是中国一代人因为出生在城镇化加速期+劳动力红利期+资产货币化期的三重叠加而获得的历史性机会。承认这一点不需要康波理论，需要的是经济发展史和制度经济学。

"Macro conditions significantly affect individual wealth accumulation" — this is true. Chinese citizens born in the right cohorts between 1980 and 2010 benefited from the triple stack of urbanization, demographic dividend, and asset monetization. Acknowledging this requires economic history and institutional economics, not Kondratieff theory.

The distinction matters:

不可证伪的命题："60 年周期决定每个人三次发财机会"。这个命题既无法在历史样本中验证（n < 5），也没有清晰的边界条件。

An unfalsifiable claim: "A 60-year cycle determines three wealth opportunities per lifetime." This cannot be verified against historical samples (n < 5), and has no clear boundary conditions.

可证伪且通过验证的命题："Shiller CAPE 估值倍数与未来 10 年股票实际回报有 −0.5 至 −0.6 的横截面相关性"[14]。这是一个可量化、可重复检验、且在 100+ 年数据上稳健的关系。

A falsifiable claim that holds up: "The Shiller CAPE has a cross-sectional correlation of −0.5 to −0.6 with subsequent 10-year real equity returns"[14]. This is quantifiable, repeatedly testable, and robust across 100+ years of data.

实践含义 Practical Takeaway

想理解宏观环境对个人财富的影响：读经济发展史、人口学、制度经济学，看 Shiller CAPE 和真实利率。"按康波周期布局人生"在数学上等同于占星术配置资产——宿命论的安慰是真的，对决策的指导是 0。

Want to understand how macro conditions shape individual wealth? Read economic history, demography, institutional economics; watch Shiller CAPE and real rates. "Allocating your life by Kondratieff phases" is mathematically equivalent to doing asset allocation by astrology — the fatalist comfort is real, the decision-making value is zero.

方法论说明与局限 Methodological Notes & Limitations

2030 窗口未检验。本文无法检验周金涛"2030 年第三次机会"的判断——它在数据上还没发生。但请注意：根据他的理论，2025 年应该已经"彻底进入第五次康波萧条阶段"[1]。2024 年至 2026 年初 A 股从 2974 点回升至 3960 点，美股从 4742 升至 6852，这与"萧条阶段"的描述并不匹配——这本身已经是对其理论框架的间接证伪。

The 2030 window cannot yet be tested — it hasn't happened. But note: Zhou's framework predicted that by 2025 the world would be "firmly in the fifth Kondratieff depression"[1]. From early 2024 to early 2026, the Shanghai Composite rose from 2974 to 3960 and the S&P 500 from 4742 to 6852. This is not what a "firmly entrenched depression" looks like — itself an indirect refutation of the framework.

价格回报 vs 总回报。本文使用价格回报，未含 A 股/美股股息或房产租金收益。包含这些会使所有起点 CAGR 系统性提高 1–2 个百分点，但相对排名不变。

Price vs. total return. Price returns only — dividends and rental income are excluded. Including them would shift all CAGRs up by 1–2 percentage points uniformly, but the relative ranking of start years is unchanged.

北京房价数据的复杂性。2008 年之前北京住宅市场分割（商品房、福利房、二手房），均价数据存在统计口径差异。本文使用国家统计局季度调查数据，但精度低于股市指数。这一不确定性不影响 2008 后的检验结论（2008 起点房价数据可靠）。

Beijing housing data caveat. Pre-2008, the Beijing residential market was segmented (commodity housing, welfare housing, resale), and price aggregates differ across measurement conventions. We use NBS quarterly survey data, but precision is lower than for equity indices. This uncertainty does not affect post-2008 conclusions (2008 housing data is reliable).

资产权重与可获得性。普通投资者无法在 1999 年初真的把全部储蓄买入北京房——一线城市住宅在那个年代对大多数人来说存在配额限制和资金门槛。本文的分析是事后假设性的，目的是检验理论本身，而非给出可执行策略。

Accessibility caveat. A typical Chinese investor in 1999 could not actually allocate all their savings to Beijing housing — there were rationing, quota, and capital barriers in tier-1 cities at the time. Our analysis is counterfactual — testing the theory's predictions, not prescribing strategy.
