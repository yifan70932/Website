China Outdoor Ski Resorts 中国户外滑雪场

~94 outdoor resorts representing the bulk of skier visits per the 2024–2025 China Ski Industry White Book. Indoor resorts are excluded. Markers colored by white-book region; size indicates resort tier (destination resort > peri-urban learning > tourist-experience). National-tier resorts (国家级滑雪旅游度假地) ringed in red. Data sources: White Book disclosures, MCT/Sports Administration designations across three batches, resort official sites.
